## Acknowledgments

zmol stands on the shoulders of:

- **[Mol\*](https://molstar.org/)** — the open WebGL rendering core that zmol uses for all 3D rendering. Maintained by an international team led by David Sehnal and the Mol\* consortium.
- **[smol6](https://github.com/sness23/smol6)** — zmol's predecessor, an Electron Mol\* wrapper that established the architectural pattern.
- **[PyMOL](https://github.com/schrodinger/pymol-open-source)** and **[ChimeraX](https://www.cgl.ucsf.edu/chimerax/)** — for being the tools whose command grammars zmol's unified parser is designed to bridge. Without 25 years of accumulated muscle memory in these two ecosystems, the whole project's premise would not exist.

zmol does not fork or modify these projects; it depends on Mol\*, and is a parallel command-compatible alternative to PyMOL and ChimeraX.
